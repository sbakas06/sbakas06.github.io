Healthy Scales

Our team project was created for an STM contest, where we developed an app using Flutter and Firebase for the database. The hardware was built with an STM32 and programmed in C++. Both the smartphone and the scale have standalone apps that allow you and your family to upload daily meals to track calorie intake. The app also offers numerous recipes, which you can follow on the scale’s screen and then upload to your daily log. This was one of the most challenging and rewarding projects I’ve ever worked on, where I gained significant knowledge in Flutter, Firebase, teamwork, and leadership.
